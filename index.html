HEB Order Checker

As I've been ordering curbside groceries from HEB, I've noticed their software has a habit of deleting items from my order, without warning, when the store runs out of something in inventory (this happens frequently with toilet paper and seasonal beer, I've noticed).

HEB Order Check is a bookmarklet that will track the contents of your order and let you know if things have been removed.

To install, drag this button to your bookmark toolbar:

HEB Order Check

To use, click "HEB Order Check" on an HEB order. Click "OK" at the first prompt. Then, it will list all of your order as additions. Click "Save This Order." When you want to check an order for changes, go to the order page and click the bookmarklet again. It will update you on changes and you may save the new order state if you wish.
